Snake Plant

$13.50

#Beginner #EasyCare

This plant is highly valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a wide range of conditions, from low to bright, indirect light. It's also known for its air-purifying qualities, as it can filter out common household toxins. Snake plants are drought-tolerant and susceptible to root rot, so it's important to avoid overwatering. Their resilience makes them a great choice for beginner plant owners.
